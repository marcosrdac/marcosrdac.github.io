A semester in a paper sheet

Posted on Thu 07 January 2021 in blog • 1 min read

It is a new year! Time for setting goals and expectations. I created a good solution to do such a task with ease some years ago which I will share with you today. It makes use of a very simple sheet you can download here. After printing, you can use it to set marks for your entire semester. The best thing about it is that you can easily see how close your goals are to your present day — something I find very motivating.

The only thing you need to set up on sheet is the initial date on cell C4 and voilà: download a PDF version (fit sheet to paper width and lower border widths so that it becomes more readable) and print its two pages on the front and back of a paper sheet. Higher paper grammature will lead to the best results, as this sheet might be carried around a lot.
